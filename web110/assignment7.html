For my first activity, I chose to clear browser cache, history, and cookies. This is important to know how to do because if you have lots of cache, it can negatively affect the performance of your device as well as your devices storage, so it is important to clear your cache periodically. You should also know how to clear your history as well if you need privacy. Clearing cookies is also important since doing that will protect your privacy by preventing websites from tracking your online activity, and lower the chance of being affected in security breaches.
